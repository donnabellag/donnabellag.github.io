Introduction

Gaetano's

We are a family-owned, Southern Italian Restaurant since 1989 that specializes in Pizza Napoletana. Our pastas, pizza dough, toppings, and appetizers are all made in house with the highest quality of imported Italian ingredients to present an authentic, modern Italian cuisine.

Target audience

The main target audience of the website is parties interested in viewing the menu or making reservations for our restaurant.

The main goal is to supply potential customers with useful information for finding our restaurant, learning about our company, making reservations, highlighting the menu, contacting us, etc.
